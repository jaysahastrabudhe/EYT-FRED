Evaluation Criteria

THE JUDGING RUBRIC

This is a game of skill, not chance. Here's exactly how your plan is scored.

20%

Problem Understanding

Has the team correctly identified the real problem in the brief, not just a surface symptom? Do they show evidence of thinking about who actually has this problem?

25%

Solution Viability

Is the proposed solution actually buildable with realistic resources? Does it solve the stated problem, not a different one?

25%

Commercial Thinking

Is there a credible path to revenue or sustainability? Have they thought about who pays, why, and how much? This is also the first tie-breaker if scores are tied.

15%

Pitch Clarity and Delivery

Can the team explain the idea clearly in the time given? Is the structure logical? Can they answer follow-up questions from judges without falling apart?

15%

Team Collaboration

Does the pitch show evidence all 3 members contributed meaningfully? Judges may ask each member a direct question. One person carrying two silent teammates scores low here.
