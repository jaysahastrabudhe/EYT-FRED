01

What This Is

The Founder's Sprint is a one-day, skill-based startup pitch challenge organized by Let's Enterprise. Participants compete in teams to solve a live business brief and pitch it to a panel of judges. This is a game of skill, not a game of chance. Outcomes are determined solely by the quality of each team's submission as assessed against a published judging rubric (see Section 6). No element of the competition is determined by lot, chance, or random draw.

06

Judging Criteria (Published Rubric)

Teams are scored against a defined rubric, published to all registered participants before the event. The rubric weights five criteria, each scored 1-5 by every judge independently:

CriterionWeight
Problem Understanding - Correctly identified the real problem in the brief, not just a surface symptom. 20%
Solution Viability - Proposed solution is actually buildable with realistic resources and solves the stated problem. 25%
Commercial Thinking - Credible path to revenue or sustainability; clear thinking on who pays, why, and how much. 25%
Pitch Clarity and Delivery - Clear explanation within the time given, logical structure, ability to handle follow-up questions. 15%
Team Collaboration - Evidence all 3 members contributed meaningfully. Judges may ask each member a direct question. 15%

Scoring is conducted solely by the judging panel using this rubric. No aspect of winner selection involves chance, lottery, or random selection.

Tie-breaker (applied only if total scores are exactly equal): First, the higher score on Commercial Thinking. If still tied, the higher score on Solution Viability. If still tied: unanimous panel discussion and re-vote by judges only. Decision is final.
